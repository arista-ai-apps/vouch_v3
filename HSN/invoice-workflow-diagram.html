Purchase Invoice Verification Workflow

Workflow for extraction, purchase register match, GSTR-2B reconciliation, HSN review, ITC/compliance checks, and reviewer actions.

flowchart TD
    A[Upload purchase invoice PDF] --> B[Classify as purchase invoice]
    B --> C[Extract header fields
Invoice no, date, supplier name, GSTIN, taxable value, GST, total]
    C --> D[Extract line items
Description, qty, rate, HSN/SAC, line value]
    D --> E{Extraction confidence OK?}
    E -- No --> E1[Send to manual review queue]
    E -- Yes --> F[Normalize data
Dates, GSTIN, invoice no, amounts]
    E1 --> F

    F --> G[Check against purchase register]
    G --> G1[Match supplier GSTIN]
    G1 --> G2[Match supplier name]
    G2 --> G3[Match invoice number]
    G3 --> G4[Match invoice date]
    G4 --> G5[Match taxable value, GST, total value]
    G5 --> G6{Register result}
    G6 -- Exact --> H[Books match]
    G6 -- Partial --> H1[Register mismatch flag]
    G6 -- Invoice only --> H2[Missing in purchase register]
    G6 -- Register only --> H3[Entry without supporting voucher]

    H --> I[Check against GSTR-2B]
    H1 --> I
    H2 --> I
    H3 --> I
    I --> I1[Match supplier GSTIN]
    I1 --> I2[Match invoice no and date]
    I2 --> I3[Match taxable value and GST amount]
    I3 --> I4{2B result}
    I4 -- Present and matched --> J[ITC likely supported]
    I4 -- Missing --> J1[Missing in 2B]
    I4 -- Tax/value mismatch --> J2[2B mismatch]
    I4 -- Period mismatch --> J3[Period review]

    J --> K[HSN review]
    J1 --> K
    J2 --> K
    J3 --> K
    K --> K1{HSN on invoice?}
    K1 -- Yes --> K2[Extract HSN at line-item level]
    K1 -- No --> K3[Recommend HSN from description
+ master mapping + vendor history]
    K2 --> K4{Confidence / consistency OK?}
    K3 --> K4
    K4 -- No --> K5[Force CA confirmation]
    K4 -- Yes --> L[Save final HSN]
    K5 --> L

    L --> M[ITC eligibility checks]
    M --> M1[Flag blocked / potentially ineligible ITC
food, personal use, restricted categories]
    M1 --> N[E-way bill checks]
    N --> N1[If applicable, verify e-way bill no]
    N1 --> O[TDS checks]
    O --> O1[Threshold and applicability review]
    O1 --> P[Duplicate / suspicious invoice checks]
    P --> P1[Duplicate invoice no, repeated amount, unusual qty/rate]
    P1 --> Q[Generate exception summary]
    Q --> R{Reviewer action}
    R -- Clear --> S[Status: Cleared]
    R -- Note --> T[Status: Cleared with notes]
    R -- Escalate --> U[Status: Exception / manager review]
    R -- Hold --> V[Status: Pending clarification]
    S --> W[Monthly verification report and dashboard]
    T --> W
    U --> W
    V --> W
Primary match keys: Supplier GSTIN, invoice number, invoice date, taxable value, GST amount, total value
HSN rule: Extract if present, suggest if missing, force review if low confidence or inconsistent
2B statuses: Present and matched, missing, mismatch, period review
Final outputs: Cleared, cleared with notes, exception, pending clarification
